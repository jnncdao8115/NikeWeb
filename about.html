About Nike

Who We Are

At Nike, we believe in the power of sport to move the world forward. For decades, we've pushed boundaries in innovation, performance, and design to empower athletes everywhere—whether they're just starting out or breaking world records.

Our Mission

Bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world.
*If you have a body, you are an athlete.

What We Stand For

• Innovation: We are constantly evolving to create cutting-edge products.
• Sustainability: We're committed to reducing our environmental impact.
• Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and stand for equality in sport and beyond.
• Performance: We build products that help athletes perform their best.
